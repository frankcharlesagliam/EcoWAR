How Pollution affects Human Health

by Frank Charles Agliam, Jona Almira Bitancor

Pollution can affect us in various ways. It can lead us to getting a cancer, parasitic or virus infections.

An example of disease that you may get when you're around a polluted area is Asthma. Asthma and other respiratory diseases are very risky and many people are prone to this diseases due to the increase of Air Pollution. Otherwise, this can be prevented if you wear masks outdoors.

In other hands, Land Pollution is a big problem that can desertify the Earth and deteriorate plants that can result to lack of Oxygen in the future. Without enough Oxygen, then we won't be able to breathe normally and can lead to death.
